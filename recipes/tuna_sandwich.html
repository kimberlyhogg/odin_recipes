Tuna Sandwich

Description

This sandwich makes a great lunch on the weekend or to pack for work. Store in the fridge until ready to eat.

Ingredients

    1 150g can tuna, drained
    30g mayonnaise
    15g relish or chopped pickles
    2 slices bread, toasted

Instruction

    Mix tuna, mayo and relish in a bowl.
    Spread tuna mix on one slice of toast.
    Top with second slice of toast. Cut in half for easier handling.
